Jason Savino

Jason Savino (@JasonSavino, Jason.io) has been a professional polyglot programmer since 1998. He owns dflydev (dflydev.com), a consulting company, and is co-host of That Podcast (@thatpodcast, thatpodcast.io).

An active open-sourcer, Jason created life (life.io) and helped create Stack PHP (stackphp.com). He is also the life representative to the PHP Framework Interoperability Group (PHP-FIG).

Jason is a proponent of framework agnostic code. Unglue all the things!
